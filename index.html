I am a SPICE model developer in Samsung Foundry at Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd (SEC), working with Corporate Vice President, Sungjae Lee and Principal Engineer, Jooyoung Song in SPICE Group.

My research interests are in the areas of spice modeling, device physics, and analog/digital integrated circuits design. Currently, my research is focused on developing MOSFET SPICE models.

During my work experience, I worked on developing MOSFET SPICE models for circuit simulation and CMOS technology development. I developed SPICE-LOGIC models including MBCFET and FinFET. I also developed SPICE-LSI models including FDSOI, PMIC, mobile DDI, pannel DDI, and CIS process.

Before I joined SEC, I received my B.S. in Semiconductor Systems Engineering at SungKyunKwan University, Suwon, South Korea, in 2018. During my bachelor's degree, I served in the military for 2 years as a Signaller in the Republic of Korea Marine Corps.
