Jaehyun Park

Hyundai and KIA R&D Center, Hyundai Motors Company

Office: Autoway Tower, Korea

Email: chang8224@hanyang.ac.kr

I am a LiDAR perception software developer in Autonomous Driving Software Development Team at Hyundai Motors Company, working on developing 3D LiDAR object classification model and object detection model for SAE autonomous level 3 and level 4 driving.

Before I joined HMC, I received my B.S. in Automotive Engineering at Automotive Control and Electronics Lab, Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2021. For my master's thesis, I proposed PCSCNet (Point Convolution and Sparse Convoltion-based Network) for real-time LiDAR point cloud semantic segmentation. I also received my B.S. in Automotive Engineering at Hanyang University, Seoul, South Korea, in 2019. In my Bachelor's dissertation, I implemented an perpendicular autonomous parking system using Infineon TC237 evaluation board.
